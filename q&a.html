Frequently Asked Questions

For all our friends and family who have lots of questions, please check out our FAQ first!

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony starts at 2pm so we would appreciate it if you could arrive at least 15 mins beforehand.

Do you have a gift list?

Your presence at the wedding is the best gift to us! However if you would like to get us something, a monetary contribution towards our honeymoon would be greatly appreciated!

If my partner is invited, do I need to separately RSVP for them?

Yes, unless if they want to do this themselves :) We need each guest to select their menu choices and let us know of any food allergies. Therefore if you and your partner are able to join us, please complete the RSVP process twice (once for yourself and once for your partner).

Where should I park?

All guests can park onsite. You can also leave your car overnight as long as they are collected by 11 am the next day (17 June 2019)
