Travelling here

By car

There is a car park available at the venue and your car can be left overnight as long as they are collected by 11am the following day (17 June 2019)

By taxi

There are various local taxi companies available but Yalding Taxis (01622 814343) and 66 Cars (01622 741617) have been recommended by the venue.

By train

The nearest train station to the venue is Yalding (5 mins taxi ride - must pre-book). Yalding itself is not on a direct line to / from London but common routes include changing at Strood or Tonbridge (South Eastern rail) - linked to London Charing Cross, London Waterloo East, London Bridge and London St. Pancras.

Accomodation

There are three Premier Inns situated nearby (Wateringbury, Maidstone central and Allington) for those staying overnight.
